Welcome to My World

Hello! I'm Enes, a dreamer, innovator, and changemaker from Iraq. My life has been a journey of exploration and passion for learning, spanning mathematics, education, technology, and social impact. Let me share my story with you.

Growing up in Baghdad, I always dreamed of making a difference. As the founder of We Change, a non-governmental organization, I've worked tirelessly to provide extracurricular opportunities for young people in Iraq. From charity events to book clubs and educational trips, We Change aims to empower youth and spark hope for a brighter future.

My journey took a new turn when I was selected to participate in the Yale Young Global Scholars program, where I delved into solving global challenges.

Beyond academics and technology, I am passionate about uniting people through shared values and diversity. My upcoming book, dedicated to showcasing Iraq's beauty and resilience, is a testament to my belief in the power of unity and understanding.

Thank you for visiting my site. I hope my story inspires you to chase your dreams and create meaningful change. Together, we can shape a better future.
