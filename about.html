Welcome to My World

Hello! I'm Enes, a passionate learner and aspiring changemaker from Iraq. Growing up in Baghdad, I’ve always been motivated by the desire to contribute to my community. As the founder of We Change, a non-governmental organization, I’ve worked to create opportunities for young people in Iraq, providing them with extracurricular activities like book clubs, charity events, and educational trips. Through this journey, I’ve learned the power of small actions to inspire hope and create positive change. I believe that change begins with understanding. That’s why I’m currently writing a book that reflects on Iraq’s culture, history, and the incredible potential within its people. By celebrating our shared values, I hope to help others see the beauty in our diversity and the strength it brings to overcoming challenges. Along the way, I’ve had the privilege of participating in various academic programs and initiatives that have broadened my understanding of global challenges and empowered me to continue striving for solutions. However, it is the everyday interactions and learning that have truly shaped my perspective on how I can contribute to a better world. Thank you for visiting my site. I hope my story encourages you to embrace your own journey, no matter how big or small. We all have a role to play in shaping a brighter future.
